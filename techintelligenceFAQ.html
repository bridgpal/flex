IT Visibility with Technology Intelligence Platform

Flexera is excited to announce enhanced Technology Intelligence Platform, the next generation data and analytics platform for IT Visibility that gives customers comprehensive visibility of their hybrid IT estate. For IT Visibility users, this new foundation delivers the next step in our continuing work to support data-driven technology decisions. Technology Intelligence Platform brings advanced analytics, extensibility, increased performance, and a flexible access to your data through APIs.

IT Visibility users will be the first to gain access to enhanced Technology Intelligence Platform as part of their existing subscription. Upon upgrade completion, access to previous IT Visibility dashboarding will be deprecated.

Technology Intelligence Platform adds the following out-of-the-box capabilities to Flexera One IT Visibility:

  • Complete picture of your technology estate with a unified view of technology assets and their relationships. 
  • Advanced analytics with flexible, customizable reporting and advanced visualization using embedded Microsoft Power BI to support data-driven decisions. 
  • Extensibility that enables you to add your own data across various types (booleans, integers, decimals, data and time) as well as user-provided property names to contextualize your inventory; use new flexible APIs to feed the enriched inventory data you need to other business systems. 
  • Faster processing and normalization of inventory data ensures reports have the freshest data and accelerate your data pipeline to ServiceNow; for customers that use the integration between Flexera and ServiceNow, ensure your CMDB stays up to date.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Note: If you are currently using Data Mashup and vulnerability capabilities, see Is there anything I can do to prepare? for specific changes.

  • Advanced analytics: The dashboarding and reports layer is changing to bring best in class data analytics capabilities with Microsoft Power BI-based reporting (see What does the upgrade process look like?). IT Visibility customers will see new, improved, out of the box reports designed to illustrate business risk and drive strategic decisions.

    Custom reporting is no longer restricted to 60,000 rows of data, and is targeted by use case, reducing the noise and the need to sort through unrelated data fields to find the relevant data for a report.

  • Extensibility: IT Visibility customers benefit from the queryable inventory API and improved performance on inventory export API with this upgrade.

    With the queryable inventory API, IT Visibility users gain targeted access to exactly the normalized inventory data they need to solve their use case, without the need to download multiple large datasets, and then join and analyze in another tool.

    The inventory export API update does not impact data shape or schema, all updates are backwards compatible, with the exception of the Vulnerability dataset (see Is there anything I can do to prepare?).

    Additionally, the existing delta capability (change log) is improved by implementing differentials (i.e. where you left off, where you need to pick up), which reduces complexity and dataset size by compacting multiple changes into a single, consolidated change for each record.

  • Faster processing and normalization: Flexera has implemented massively scalable and highly performant architecture update, significantly enhancing data processing and correlation. With this upgrade, the average data processing time for IT Visibility customers has been reduced from 24-48 hours to 2-4 hours from source to customer.
  • Accelerated data pipeline to ServiceNow: Flexera Integration app for ServiceNow benefits from improved performance and export capabilities. No rework is required for customers who have implemented the integration already and there is no change to data import to Flexera from ServiceNow.

There is no change in how data is brought into IT Visibility. Source connections remain the same. Technopedia normalization processes are unaffected.

This upgrade will be made available to IT Visibility customers beginning September 2024. Flexera will contact IT Visibility customers with a targeted date for upgrade during this time. Current IT Visibility dashboards will be deprecated for all users upon close of upgrade period.

Nothing at this time, Flexera will contact you with your upgrade details.

Some steps you can take between now and your upgrade:

  • If you are using Data Mashup in the IT Visibility UI, please take this opportunity to migrate to our contextual API capability. For more information, see Contextual Data Store API (Data Mashup UI Replacement).

  • If you are using vulnerability data outside IT Visibility, please see Technology Intelligence Platform Datasets for backwards incompatible changes .
  • If you are using out-of-the-box and custom reports, see the Technology Intelligence Platform Reports in Getting Started: IT Visibility with Technology Intelligence Platform.

Once you’ve received this communication (including FAQ), your Customer Success or Account Manager will reach out to schedule a call within 5 business days, explaining the upgrade process in detail. Expect the following:

  • A target date for the upgrade to take place
  • Further detailed supporting documentation
  • A follow-up email from your account manager confirming next steps.

Next steps typically include:

  • Entitlement to Technology Intelligence Platform access confirmation
  • Technology Intelligence Platform enhancement turned on in your environment
  • Access to previous data visualization and processing support to be turned off 6 weeks after Technology Intelligence Platform enhancement is enabled in customer environment.
  • Upon receiving access:
    • This is a great time to check out the newly upgraded reports and start making updates to your environment, as needed.
    • Try out different workflows.
    • See Getting Started: IT Visibility with Technology Intelligence Platform.
    • Ask us questions—our dedicated team will be readily available to support your upgrade support@flexera.com.
    • Expect a reminder 2-to-3 weeks before existing IT Visibility capabilities are disabled (dashboards and Data Mashup UI).

You will receive an email notice from Flexera approximately 1 month prior to your upgrade date to the enhanced IT Visibility (including this FAQ). The notice will indicate the targeted upgrade date and additional steps.

Upgrade for Technology Intelligence Platform enhancements will be instantaneous in your IT Visibility environment(s); you will have access to your existing dashboards for 6 weeks until they are automatically turned off (see above section for more detail).

Nothing will happen to your data. It will continue to be available on the legacy dashboards for six weeks after your upgrade date. The new reporting capabilities will become available at upgrade. After the six-week window, the existing IT Visibility dashboards will be removed, and you will still see your data available in the updated reporting and analytics views.

If you have previously implemented our Data Mashup features for your own organizational contextual data, this feature will be moving from the current user interface to the existing contextual API. More details are available above in:

  • Is there anything I can do to prepare?
  • Contextual Data Store API (Data Mashup UI Replacement) in Getting Started: IT Visibility with Technology Intelligence Platform.

Your current custom and out-of-the-box dashboards will still be available for 6 weeks after Technology Intelligence Platform enhancements have been turned on. During this timeframe, you will be able to recreate your custom views in the new reporting capability. Out-of-the-box dashboards will be replaced with new, enhanced out-of-the-box reports.

At the end of this timeframe, access to all previous IT Visibility dashboarding capabilities will be removed.
